D E Z I N O

Student Design Collective

About

DEZINO is a student collective comprised of over a dozen architectural, landscape, and industrial designers. We utilize our variety of experiences and insights to generate design solutions that are user-centered, environmentally considerate, and universally accessible. DEZINO maintains a non-hierarchical internal structure; our strength comes from the ability of all members to contribute leadership and input in varying capacities from project to project.
